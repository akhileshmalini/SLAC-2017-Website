The one and only 24 hour Hackathon of Amrita School of Engineering - SLAC 2017!
Stay Late And Code(SLAC) brings together developers, designers and coders to create, innovate and put them into action.

Be it a unique application that lets you play 3D tic-tac-toe, or even the next big social network, the hackers build the most fantastic products overnight, anything from useful-to-hilarious products. Join us for this carnival of ideas and walk away with your mind blown!
